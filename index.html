About me

About Me

Hello! I am Akash Chandra Behera, better known as ACB or Akash. I am a Doctoral Researcher at the Department of Physical Chemistry at The Fritz Haber Institute of the Max Planck Society . I am working with the Lattice Dynamics Group, where my role is to setup and perform Two-Color Experiments with the Fritz Haber Insitute Free Electron Laser. I completed my Bachleor and Master of Science from the Indian Institute of Science Education and Research (IISER) Kolkata, where I completed my Master Thesis on Laser Physics under Prof. Nir Davidson at the Coupled Lasers Lab of the Weizmann Institute of Science in Israel.

My current research lies at the intersection of Optics and Condensed Matter Physics. Specifically, I aim to utilize the FHI-FEL to conduct Two-Color Spectroscopy and Four-Wave Mixing experiments, exploring light-matter interactions at an advanced level
